My Addin App

Version 1.0.0

Description

This is a powerful image editing addin that allows you to easily add stickers, shapes, and text to your canvas. Enhance your creativity with this lightweight tool designed to make image manipulation simple and fun!

Features

  • Add stickers, shapes, and text with one click
  • Convert images to PNG or JPEG formats
  • Resize, rotate, and customize objects on the canvas
